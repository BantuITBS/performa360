RACI Framework: Clarity in Action

Performa360 uses the RACI framework to ensure every stakeholder knows their role, streamlining workflows and enhancing accountability:

  • Responsible: HR Admins configure settings; Employees complete assessments; IT manages integrations.
  • Accountable: Managers approve goals and reviews; HR oversees adoption.
  • Consulted: Team Leads refine criteria; Employees shape feedback; Consultants align with best practices.
  • Informed: Executives receive reports; Team Members get updates; Compliance teams ensure regulatory adherence.
